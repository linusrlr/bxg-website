Our Story

MORE THAN A GYM.
A LEGACY REBORN.

Founded in 2003 as BXG Boxing & Fitness, we've been Singapore's home for authentic Muay Thai for over two decades. Now reborn as BXG Muay Thai Academy, our members stepped up to carry the legacy forward — with fresh energy, a new name, and the same uncompromising standards.

Led by coaches Martin and Damien, we run over 30 classes per week — from fundamentals and pad work to advanced application. Located in the heart of Tanjong Katong, 7 days a week.

New Here?

YOUR FIRST SESSION

Never thrown a punch? Perfect. Here's what to expect.

Book Online

Head to our booking page and pick a Fundamentals class. All bookings are through VibeFam — quick and easy.

What to Bring

Comfortable workout clothes, a towel, and water. We have gloves and wraps you can borrow. Arrive 10 minutes early.

Your First Class

Fundamentals classes cover core techniques with fitness circuits. Coaches guide you through every step — no experience needed.

Keep Going

Talk to Martin or Damien about your goals. They'll help you choose the right path from fundamentals through to advanced training.
